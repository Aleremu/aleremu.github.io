Tabakalera

An Infinite Destination for Your Imagination

Developed in collaboration with The Lion and MUY, this project features four augmented reality experiences for Tabakalera's campaign. By scanning QR codes on custom landing pages, users unlock unique digital worlds representing different artists' visions of the cultural space.

The solution utilizes WebAR technology (Unity, C#, WebGL) to ensure maximum accessibility through standard web browsers, eliminating the need for additional app installations. The development is fully responsive across multiple systems and devices, and supports multiple languages.

Casa del Libro

A Unos Libros de Distancia

Web & AI Experience developed in collaboration with The Lion and This is Libre, "A Unos Libros de Distancia" is an award-winning digital experience for Casa del Libro that reimagines book discovery. Moving beyond traditional genres, the platform connects users' life aspirations with curated reading itineraries.

Users simply state a goal such as "I want to be a better leader" and the system generates a personalized selection of books to help them achieve it.

The solution bridges creative vision with technical precision. It features a custom AI-driven recommendation engine that semantically maps abstract human goals to specific book catalogs. To ensure the highest quality, these AI-generated itineraries were reviewed and validated by a team of literary experts before being presented to the user.

On the frontend, the platform delivers a polished, interactive experience, utilizing smooth motion design and instant search capabilities to transform complex data processing into an elegant, intuitive user journey.

Antena 3

Emparejados

Developed for Antena 3's prime-time slot, "Emparejados" is a dynamic TV show where we engineered the complete software ecosystem to manage real-time studio content and interactive game mechanics.

The platform synchronized visual assets across massive studio videowalls, managed the game logic for contestants, and integrated seamlessly with studio lighting systems.

The project required a robust, fail-safe architecture capable of operating live. We created a centralized control dashboard that allowed the production team to orchestrate the entire show flow instantly triggering events and updating scores.

On the studio floor, the system provided dedicated, synchronized interfaces for the contestants, ensuring a fluid and engaging experience for the participants and the audience.
